# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 2026*

## Overview

Frameglyph is a desktop application that runs entirely on your local machine. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

Frameglyph does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All project files, videos, and annotations remain on your local device.

## Network Access

Frameglyph does not require an internet connection to operate. No data is sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

When distributed via Steam, the Steam client may collect usage data according to [Valve's Privacy Policy](https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/). This is outside the scope of Frameglyph itself.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@frameglyph.com.
